eMOLT Weekly Update 2023-01-06

Weekly Recap

Happy New Year!

This week has been a mix of meetings with partner organizations, planning for the upcoming biogeochemical network workshop at WHOI, digging out from the holiday back log, and even hopping on a few boats.

We heard from both Maine Dept of Marine Resources and Mass Dept of Marine Fisheries colleaques concerning their respective bottom temperature collections. In the the ME-DMR case, we received about a dozen multi-month time series from their 2021 Ventless-Trap program and added these data to the master database. We started discussions with MA-DMF to update the time series from their multi-decadal ship wreck time series, the longest running bottom-temperature data in the Northeast.

We also met with the crew over at the Commercial Fisheries Research Foundation to discuss our efforts to help QA/QC their data and make it more widely available to the scientific community.
